You can use some of the resources ExPex provides to simplify things.  First,
you can use \verb!\tspace! to gather the dimensions into the heading so that it is
easier to adjust the settings to what you want.

\ex[dima=1.4em,dimb=3em]
\vtop{\halign{%
   #\hfil& \tspace[dima] \ipb #\hfil& \tspace[dimb] \ipb #\hfil& \tspace[dimb] `#'\hfil\cr
& \omit \tspace[dima] \hwit{singular}&  \omit\tspace[dimb]\hwit{plural}&
   \omit\tspace[dimb]\hwit{gloss}\cr
\noalign{\smallskip}
a.& j\`Oh\'O& w\`\i\'e& horse\cr
b.& kp\'O\`N& b\'ER\`a& big\cr
c.& s\'ann\'\i\'\i& s\'al\'\i m\'a& gold\cr
d.& t\^O& t\'a\'a& kind\cr
}}\xe

You can use \verb!\tl! and \verb!\nl! to make labeling automatic.  You can also use the fact
that \verb!\tspace! with no optional argument defaults to \verb!\tspace[dima]!.

\ex[dima=1.4em,dimb=3em]
\vtop{\labels\halign{%
   \tl #\hfil& \tspace \ipb #\hfil& \tspace[dimb] \ipb #\hfil& \tspace[dimb] `#'\hfil\cr
\nl & \omit \tspace \hwit{singular}&  \omit\tspace[dimb]\hwit{plural}&
   \omit\tspace[dimb]\hwit{gloss}\cr
\noalign{\smallskip}
& j\`Oh\'O& w\`\i\'e& horse\cr
& kp\'O\`N& b\'ER\`a& big\cr
& s\'ann\'\i\'\i& s\'al\'\i m\'a& gold\cr
& t\^O& t\'a\'a& kind\cr
}}\xe

If you use the same dima and dimb over many examples, setting them can be global.

\newenvironment{glex}
   {\vtop\bgroup\labels\halign\bgroup
      \tl ##\hfil& \tspace \ipb ##\hfil& \tspace[dimb] \ipb ##\hfil&
         \tspace[dimb] `##'\hfil\cr
   \nl & \omit \tspace \hwit{singular}&  \omit\tspace[dimb]\hwit{plural}&
      \omit\tspace[dimb]\hwit{gloss}\cr
   \noalign{\smallskip}}
   {\egroup\egroup}

\ex
\begin{glex}

& s\'ann\'\i\'\i& s\'al\'\i m\'a&     gold\cr
& t\^O&           t\'a\'a&            kind\cr
\end{glex}

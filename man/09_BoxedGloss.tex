
\section Boxed glosses

Sometimes it is desirable to build a gloss with an hsize smaller than
the current hsize.

\lingset{glbreaking=true}

\ex
\baselineskip=18pt
\begingl[autoglskip=true,extraglskip=4pt]
\gla Ti ma'a'\~nao hao kumuentusi ni h\'ayiyi ha'.//
\glb not agr.afraid you Infin.speak.to not anyone Emp//
\endgl
\xe

\ex
\vtop{\hsize=2.5in \parindent=0pt \leftskip=0pt
\baselineskip=18pt
\begingl[autoglskip=true,extraglskip=4pt]
\gla Ti ma'a'\~nao hao kumuentusi ni h\'ayiyi ha'.//
\glb not agr.afraid you Infin.speak.to not anyone Emp//
\endgl
}\xe
